How It Works

The process

Step 01

Brief

We talk about your vision, audience, and goals. You send across the raw footage and any references you love.

Step 02

First Cut

I structure and cut the edit, focusing on pacing, narrative flow, and rhythm before applying any finishing touches.

Step 03

Revisions

You review the edit and send feedback. We refine together until it feels exactly right — up to two rounds included.

Step 04

Delivery

Final export delivered in your preferred format and resolution, ready to publish, screen, or share.
